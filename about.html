About Me

Yooooooooooo, this is Adedamola some say damzy, I specialize in intricate pencil and pen illustrations that invite viewers to explore the depths of introspection.

I'm just a chill guy so my inspiration comes from all over the place, experiences, music i hear, social media , other artists. I started out not copying ;) but replicating works i see on the internet, i want to take the next step and enhance my work.

To provoke thoughts and carry universal themes, every piece of art will have a meaning. to be a bridge what we say/see to what we actually feel, leaving a mark on those who experience my stuff.
